# Changelog

## Version 2.1 — The Living Field Archive

This release marks the transition from a photography portfolio to a living archive of observation.

### Added
- Six permanent Story pages with complete narratives and “What Stayed With Me” reflections.
- “Return to the Front Porch” navigation.
- Permanent Curator’s Ledger accession numbers for the twelve current observations.
- Article, CollectionPage, ImageObject, WebSite, Person, and CreativeWorkSeries structured data.
- Expanded Open Graph and social-card metadata.
- Updated sitemap containing every new Story URL.
- More descriptive image alternative text and accessible story breadcrumbs.

### Refined
- Renamed the first observation to “Held Together by Morning.”
- Corrected “Two Ways to Wait” to describe a young boy rather than a fisherman.
- Reframed Stories around memory rather than Field Notes.
- Updated the homepage language to introduce The Living Field Archive.

## Version 2.1.1 — Curated Field Notes

- Added complete, collapsible Field Notes to all twelve observations in “Things I Noticed.”
- Added a closing observation question to each entry while preserving the gallery’s visual breathing room.
- Confirmed Google Analytics 4 measurement ID `G-Z0NKE904WG` is present on every HTML page.
- Preserved collection numbers, structured data, Story pages, and social metadata from Version 2.1.

## Version 2.1.2 — Field Notes Interaction Hotfix

- Separated the Field Notes controls from the photograph lightbox.
- Clicking “Read the Field Notes” or the plus/minus control now only expands or collapses the written notes.
- Clicking the photograph continues to open the enlarged image viewer.
