About the photographer

A way of seeing.

Photography rooted in curiosity, observation, and the belief that beauty is not limited to extraordinary places.

I’m Lauren—Lo—the photographer behind l0sav Photography.

My work moves between nature, landscapes, local landmarks, portraits, travel, and experimental imagery. The subjects vary because people connect with different things. What ties the work together is attention.

Different subjects. One perspective.

A butterfly, a bridge, a weathered tree, an industrial landmark, or a quiet road can each carry beauty, history, and feeling.

Northern California roots

Much of my work reflects Crockett, the Carquinez Strait, Contra Costa County, and the wider landscapes of Northern California.
